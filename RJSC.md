**For a Bangladeshi software company (software farm), here are the key CRM/ERP features needed for RJSC compliance and easy audit readiness (monthly, quarterly, or annual).**

RJSC (Registrar of Joint Stock Companies) requires **mandatory statutory audit** for all companies. You must submit audited financials (Balance Sheet + Profit & Loss) within 30 days of AGM, and Annual Return (Schedule X) within 21 days.

A good **CRM with strong Finance + Accounting modules** (or integrated ERP like Odoo, Biznify, PrismERP, Focus i, etc.) is essential for software companies in Bangladesh. Pure sales CRM is not enough — you need **Financial CRM / ERP capabilities**.

### 1. Core Modules You Need in CRM/ERP

| Module | Why Needed for RJSC & Audit | Key Options/Features |
|--------|-----------------------------|----------------------|
| **Client & Project Management** | Track software projects, milestones, retainers | Project-wise revenue recognition, time & material billing, fixed price contracts |
| **Invoicing & Billing** | Accurate revenue tracking | Auto invoice generation, VAT (15%) support, multiple currencies (USD/BDT), partial invoicing, credit notes |
| **Expense Management** | All costs must be properly categorized for audit | Employee expenses, vendor bills, project expenses, bank reconciliation, receipt upload |
| **Accounting & General Ledger** | Core for Balance Sheet & P&L | Double-entry accounting, Chart of Accounts (BD specific), Journal entries, Trial Balance, Depreciation |
| **Bank Reconciliation** | Mandatory for audit trail | Auto match bank statements, multiple bank accounts |
| **Tax & VAT Compliance** | NBR + RJSC | VAT (BIN) integration, TDS, Income Tax calculation, e-Tax return ready reports |
| **Financial Reporting** | Ready for monthly/quarterly/annual audit | Balance Sheet, Profit & Loss, Cash Flow Statement, Trial Balance, Aged Receivable/Payable, Custom reports |
| **Audit Trail & History** | RJSC auditor requirement | Every transaction log with user, date, time (immutable) |

### 2. Specific Features for Monthly/Quarterly Audit Readiness

- **Closing Period Feature** — Ability to close monthly/quarterly books (prevent editing old transactions after approval).
- **Multi-Period Reporting** — Generate P&L and Balance Sheet for any period (1 month, 3 months, FY).
- **Revenue Recognition Rules** — Important for software companies (milestone, subscription, time & material).
- **Fixed Asset Register** — Track laptops, servers, software licenses with depreciation (as per BAS/IAS).
- **Employee Timesheet Integration** — Link developer hours to projects for accurate costing.
- **Document Management** — Store contracts, invoices, expense receipts, bank statements (audit ready).
- **Dashboard & Analytics** — Real-time view of revenue, expenses, profit by project/client.
- **Role-based Access** — Accountant, Auditor, Director views (for internal control).

### 3. Recommended CRM/ERP Options for Bangladesh Software Companies

- **Odoo** — Excellent for software firms (Project + Accounting + CRM integrated).
- **Biznify / PrismERP / Focus i** — Locally popular, strong VAT & Bangladesh compliance.
- **Zoho One / Zoho Books + CRM** — Good if you want cloud + affordable.
- **QuickBooks + Custom CRM** — Strong accounting, but may need integration.
- **TallyPrime** (with customization) — Very common in BD.

### 4. Bonus Recommendations

1. Choose a system that supports **Bangladesh Chart of Accounts** and **VAT rules**.
2. Ensure it has **strong reporting engine** — auditors love clean Trial Balance + supporting ledgers.
3. Enable **monthly closing process** so your books are always 95% ready for audit.
4. Integrate with **bank feeds** (if available) and payment gateways (bKash, Nagad, SSLCommerz).
5. Keep proper **supporting documents** attached to every transaction.
